Delete Account - EduNova AI

Users can request deletion of their EduNova AI account and associated data.

How To Delete Your Account

  1. Open EduNova AI app
  2. Go to Profile → Delete Account
  3. Confirm deletion request

Or contact: support@edunovaai.com

Deleted Data

  • User profile
  • AI chat history
  • Uploaded PDFs
  • Notes and quizzes

Some security logs may be retained for up to 90 days.
